YOUR DAILY DOSE OF PURR AND CAFFEINE STARTS MEOW!~

Catfeine is a cozy, cat-themed cafe that blends the comforting aroma of freshly brewed coffee with the soothing presence of friendly feline companions. Designed for coffee lovers and cat enthusiasts alike, Catfeine offers a unique experience where customers can relax, sip their favorite drinks, and interact with adoptable rescue cats in a safe, relaxing environment. It’s more than just a cafe – it’s a sanctuary for both people and cats

Mission

  • To provide a warm and welcoming space where guests can unwind with high-quality coffee and connect with cats in need of love and care.
  • To support animal welfare by partnering with local shelters for cat adoption and education.
  • To foster a community that values relaxation, kindness, and responsible pet ownership.
  • To inspire positive mental well-being through a unique café experience that soothes the soul—one cup and one purr at a time.

Vision

To become a leading cat cafe that promotes wellness, compassion, and community through the joy of coffee and the comfort of cats
