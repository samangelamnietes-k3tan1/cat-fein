WHAT WILL BE THE STREAM DESIGN OF PODCATS?

Here, we will be presenting our Stream Design for our first streaming podcast with the PodCats. Start watching it and ENJOY!

Let's discuss the stream design's overlay, alerts, and webcam borders!

Thumbnail: The thumbnail showed an elegant and theatrical approach to increase affinity with our viewers and listeners.
Intro: The "Getting Ready" screen would be helpful for both the streamers and viewers to prepare before the stream.
Alerts:
- New Subscribe: Shout out to our new subscribers!
- New Follower: Shout out to our new followers as well!
- New Donate: If you have spare money on your wallet, please help the podcats to buy their cat food! :3
Overlay 1 The first overlay has 5 webcams for each member of podcats.
Overlay 2: The second overlay only has 1 webcam for slide show presentation or the webcam of one of the members.
Rest:The "Be Right Back" screen allows the streamers and viewers to take a break. It can be used as well when there is a technical difficulty with the stream or other interferences.
Outro: This screen typically informs the viewers that the stream already ended.


NEXT UP! As our dear viewers can see our stream design, its color palette consists of a combination of Dark Green, Orange, Yellow, and Dark Gray. You can look at the guide below for reference.

Supposedly, Dark Green means Nature, Growth, and Wealth. Whilst Orange, express Energy, Happiness, and Vitality. Of course, Yellow would be Happiness, Hope, and Deceit. Then combining these 3 colors with Dark Gray where it expresses a Moody, Conservativity, and Formality.
